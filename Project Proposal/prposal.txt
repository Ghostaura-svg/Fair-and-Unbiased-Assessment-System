\section{Project Objectives}
The primary objectives of the Fair and Unbiased Assessment System are:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item To develop a web-based system which can mark quizzes and assignments with the help of artificial intelligence and modern technologies.
    \item To minimize human errors and biases in the grading process, making it fair and transparent.
    \item To reduce teachers' workload and make the grading process fast and clear.
    \item To record student performance and assessments in a database for easy tracking.
    \item To allow faculty members to review, verify, and edit AI-generated results.
    \item To provide transparency and academic integrity, making the education system more reliable and trustworthy.
\end{enumerate}
